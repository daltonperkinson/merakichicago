Our Story

Integrative wellness for Chicago's furry kids

Since 2005, Wigglyville has been providing leading products and health information right here in the neighborhood. Our mission is simple and unwavering: offer products that promote a positive lifestyle, contribute to long healthy lives, and support a sustainable world.

We are extremely selective about what we carry — choosing quality for health, sturdiness for longevity, and eco-conscious materials because we believe a healthy pet needs a healthy planet.

Founded with Heart

It started with Riley.

"He set me on my journey to transition careers into the pet industry. He is with me always and still guides my thoughts."

A rescued puppy who changed everything. That story became Wigglyville — a women-owned, veteran-led independent boutique that has been a corner of this community for over 20 years.

We invest heavily in our team's knowledge and professionalism, sell only what we believe in, and show up for the neighborhood that shows up for us.

In-Store Services

We're here for the whole life of your pet

Beyond the shelf — our knowledgeable staff provides hands-on services that make Wigglyville more than a store.

Professional Grooming

In-store grooming by our experienced team, including our beloved groomer Hollis. Your pet leaves looking — and feeling — their best.

Nutrition Consultation

Our staff are trained to help you navigate food choices based on your pet's breed, age, health conditions, and dietary needs. No upselling — just honest guidance.

Microchipping

Safe, quick, and permanent identification for your pet. Walk in, no appointment needed. One of the most important things you can do for your furry kid's safety.

Wellness Exams

Basic pet physical and wellness assessments by knowledgeable staff, with referrals to our trusted vet network when needed.

Community & Events
